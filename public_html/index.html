Roopkotha

app allows you and your children to listen to the Bangla fairy tales

The Roopkotha product is an initiative of Kite Bangladesh Ltd. is to revive the Bangla fairy tale, both traditional and literary, with the mwand of ICT. The product will revitalize the power of fairy tales via mobile apps.The Apps have short audio versions of the stories for listening over the mobile phone. Clients can download stories both in reading and listening format through android mobile phone.
